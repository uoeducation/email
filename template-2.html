LITERACY LEADERSHIP IN THE AGE OF COMMON CORE
This summer’s institute will focus on strategies for building leadership and capacity for literacy instruction in K-12 given the challenges of the Common Core State Standards and the new SmarterBalanced assessments. Hear from nationally renowned researchers who have been integrally involved with districts and schools facing these challenges, and Oregon school district leaders who will share their leadership strategies around the Common Core, including what has and has not worked so far.
