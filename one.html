Finding Foundation Funding

A workshop presented by Katy Lenn and Some Otherfolk who say additional things

Katy will provide an overview of the Foundation database available through the UO library. She plans to talk about strategies for accessing Foundation funding to support research and outreach activities.

The event is open to all faculty and graduate students. Light refreshments are to be provided.
